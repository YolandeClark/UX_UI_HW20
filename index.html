Mobile App Case Study

Adventure Hub

Acting as a product designer for a new travel startup. The startup plans to launch the app once the global pandemic ends. The startupâ€™s goal is to build a modern-day mobile app that helps people plan their next trip, post pandemic

What I Did

As part of user research I teamed up with one other person to establish a research question and objectives to be reached through interviews.I then conducted user interviews and combined my finding with my research partner. Together we created an affinity diagram, synthesised our findings and developed an empathy map. With this I then created a user persona. From there I worked alone in the Ideation and definition phase. I brainstormed ideas from the research and ranked these in a priority matrix. I then established a value proposition followed by the user journey and storyboard. This set me up to then create a paper prototype which I then made as a wireframe in Figma. From here I wasable to conduct user tests. In my last week I created a mid definition, iteration of my prototype.

See My Research

Research

Our team began planning the interview by establishing the research question followed by three objectives. Following these objectives we crafted twelve questions to be asked of our interviewees. Consideration to the original research question was present throughout this process. The next step was to find 4 to 6 participants to be interviewed. People who consider travel as a significant part of their life were our targets to be interviewed. This resulted in 5 successful interviews with a range of potential users.

User Testing and Iteration

User Tests Key Findings After conducting around three User tests I was able to gather information of where problems were encountered with the prototype. The Log in / Sign up process proved to be confusing along with some navigation and UI issues around the homepage and Covid Search panel. Priority of what iterations must be made was measured on this 2x2 Prioritisation Matrix. Navigation and refining the Log In/ Sign Up process was the highest priority. Iterations Made based on User Tests; Refinement of the Login and Sign up process to eliminate confusion, Adding extra screens to facilitate the extra steps needed to enable the user to sign in and customise with ease and The Covid Search feature was elaborated upon to become a more meaningful feature
